About us

Welcome to Chouk Physiotherapy Clinic! We are a small Physiotherapy clinic located in Ottawa, Ontario.

We are dedicated to providing the best care to our customers. Our physiotherapists will assess and treat your injury for a very affordable price. Our focus is to be a clinic that provides the best treatment in the Ottawa area.

Services


Service

Description
Massage Therapy The hands-on manipulation of the soft tissues of the body with the purpose of optimizing health. These are the connective tissue, joints, tendons, muscles, and ligaments.
Acupuncture The insertion of very thin needles through the skin at strategic points on the body.
Aqua Therapy Treatment and exercises that takes place in water for relaxation, fitness or physical rehabilitation.
Yoga Therapy Therapy that uses yoga postures, breathing exercises, meditation, and guided imagery to improve mental and physical health.
Vestibular Rehab Treatment involving specific exercises to improve balance function, decrease dizziness/vertigo symptoms, and increase general activity levels.
